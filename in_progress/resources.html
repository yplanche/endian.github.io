Data sets

These data sets are feely available for download and use. 
But keep in mind that we put quite a bit of time and effort in putting these together, so please don't forget to acknowledge us in your publications. Alternatively, we are always keen to collaborate, so why not contact us and see if we could not work together. 

Rare earth elements

A global dataset of dissolved REE concentration and eNd isotopic ratios in seawater. 

Thorium 230, 232 and Protactinium 231

A global collection of dissolved 232Th, 230Th and 231Pa abundances.

Beryllium 10

A global dataset of dissolved 10Be data in seawater.

Pb concentration and Pb isotopes

A global dataset of dissolved Pb concentrations and available 204Pb, 206Pb, 207Pb and 208Pb abundances.
