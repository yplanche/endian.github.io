Yves Plancherel

Yves is the head of the ENDIAN group. He has broad interests and benefits from a diverse background. Yves worked on coastal microbial water quality issues, global carbon cycling, climate model intercomparison and on modelling the oceanic distribution of various metals and isotopes.
