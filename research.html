Thorium isotopes

Owning to its propensity to adsorb onto surfaces, Th is a great element to learn about the role of particles in the ocean. By coupling radioactive and stable Th isotopes, one can estimate particle sinking rates and elemental input fluxes to the ocean.

The Pb cycle

Pb is one of the great geophysical experiment. Massive amouts of Pb have been released to the environment when leaded gasoline was being used (from the 1920s). Where is this Pb now? Pb isotopes also carry regional signatures. Can these isotopic signatures help us reconstruct  fluxes  and the fate of Pb in the ocean?
